About Us

Our Mission

Founded in 1992 by a former equipment sales business owner, the goal of Food Equipment Service became to give honest, reliable, timely service to those who needed commercial cooking repairs. Now we have been an industry leader in providing prompt professional service and advancing technologies by being one of the first to have technicians trained to use on board computers. From their trucks they can now look at diagrams and select parts for your specific model of equipment, print out clear easy to read invoices, and them dispatch themselves to the next job. No more bottlenecking of service techs calling into the office to close their tickets or waiting to see where their next call is. Now the service tech is more efficient making them happier, and making you happier. This increased efficency is also helping to make you happier by helping us to keep our labor rates as competitive as they can be. More money in your bottom line means you have more bottom line to help make your customers happier too.
